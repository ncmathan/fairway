The Problem

Golf in Singapore is rising.
But who gets to play?

Traditional entry points assume you already have access, confidence, and social connections. Most beginners drop off before they even start.

💸

High Upfront Costs

Club memberships, equipment, and per-session fees create a steep financial barrier before you've even swung a club.

😰

Intimidating Culture

Golf clubs carry a reputation for exclusivity. Beginners feel out of place, underprepared, and unwelcome.

🤝

Reliance on Networks

Most people enter golf through family, varsity teams, or corporate connections — leaving everyone else without a starting point.

📚

Lessons Teach Technique, Not Entry

"Golf lessons teach swing and form. They don't teach entry." — There's no structured onboarding for social confidence and real-world fluency.

"I wanted to start playing but I didn't know anyone. Private lessons were expensive and I felt like I'd embarrass myself. I just gave up."
— A typical beginner's story
The Solution

We teach entry.
Not just technique.

Fairway First is a structured onboarding service built specifically for young adult beginners. We guide you through your first month in golf — combining technical fundamentals with social fluency, inside a small, friendly cohort.

A progressive curriculum designed so you never feel thrown in the deep end.

Driving Range Games
